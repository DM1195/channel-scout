# Test Plan Schema

## Channels Being Tested
List of channel names (max 2).

## For Each Channel

### Sample Posts
2 draft posts, written in founder's voice, native to the platform format.

### Cadence
Posts per week + best times based on channel intel.

### Kill Criteria
Specific numbers that trigger a pivot decision. Example:
- If [metric] stays below [number] after [N] posts → stop this channel

### Success Signal
What does "working" look like at 7 days?

## Coaching Mode
Selected mode: automatic | on-open | manual
Setup details (cron time, launchd path, or "none").
